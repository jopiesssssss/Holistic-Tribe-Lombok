Holistic discovery · Lombok

Find holistic facilitators, retreats and soulful experiences across Lombok.

A calm place for travelers, seekers and locals to discover yoga, breathwork, meditation, sound healing, bodywork, ceremonies, retreats and conscious community on the island.

Holistic Tribe wants to give facilitators around the world a beautiful podium to be seen and connected with the people who are looking for their work. Lombok is the first chapter: a powerful place to begin, where more travelers, seekers and conscious communities are arriving and where more holistic experiences are unfolding.
