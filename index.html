Holistic discovery · Lombok

Find holistic facilitators, retreats and soulful experiences across Lombok.

A calm place for travelers, seekers and locals to discover yoga, breathwork, meditation, sound healing, bodywork, ceremonies, retreats and conscious community on the island.

Holistic Tribe Lombok is a non-profit community initiative created for this island: a calm guide that helps travelers, seekers and locals discover the yoga, healing, retreats, ceremonies and conscious spaces that make Lombok so special. By bringing these offerings together beautifully, we want to support local facilitators and invite more holistically interested visitors to experience Lombok with depth, care and respect.
