About Holistic Tribe Lombok

Giving holistic facilitators a beautiful place to be found.

Holistic Tribe Lombok is the first local chapter of a larger vision: a global platform where travelers, seekers and locals can discover holistic facilitators and experiences in one calm, trustworthy place.

The vision

A global platform, starting in Lombok.

Holistic Tribe wants to give holistic facilitators around the world a beautiful podium to be seen, found and connected with the people who are looking for their work.

Lombok is a powerful place to begin: a beautiful island where more travelers, seekers and conscious communities are arriving, and where more holistic experiences are unfolding.

Holistic Tribe Lombok aims to become a calm home base where visitors can discover the island’s holistic offerings with more ease and trust.

Why this exists

Bringing scattered information together.

Travelers often arrive in Lombok looking for yoga, healing sessions, breathwork, meditation, ceremonies, bodywork or retreats — but the information is often scattered across Instagram, WhatsApp groups, flyers and word of mouth.

Holistic Tribe Lombok brings these offerings together in one gentle platform, while giving facilitators, studios and retreat spaces a clear place to share what they offer.

Our intention is simple: make holistic discovery easier for visitors, and make visibility easier for the people offering this work.

For visitors

A calm discovery guide for yoga, healing, bodywork, ceremonies, retreats and conscious community in Lombok.

For facilitators

A simple, elegant place to share your work, links, location, events and profile with people who are actively searching.

For the future

A repeatable model that can later expand to other holistic destinations around the world.

Next step

Help shape the Lombok chapter.

Have a suggestion, correction, event or facilitator to add? Reach out and help make the platform more complete.
