About Holistic Tribe Lombok

Giving holistic facilitators a beautiful place to be found.

Holistic Tribe Lombok is a calm discovery platform for the island’s holistic side: yoga, breathwork, meditation, healing, sound journeys, bodywork, ceremonies, retreats and conscious community.

The intention

A beautiful home for holistic Lombok.

Holistic Tribe Lombok was created to make the island’s holistic offerings easier to find, easier to trust and easier to connect with.

Lombok has a special energy: spacious, natural, soulful and still more quiet than many other well-known wellness destinations. More travelers are arriving here not only for beaches and surf, but also for inner work, conscious community, yoga, healing, retreats and a slower way of living.

Our intention is to help that movement grow in a grounded way — by giving facilitators, studios and retreat spaces a beautiful place to be seen, and by helping visitors discover experiences that bring them closer to the real spirit of Lombok.

Why this exists

More conscious travelers, more meaningful connections.

Many visitors arrive in Lombok looking for yoga, healing sessions, breathwork, meditation, ceremonies, bodywork or retreats — but the information is often scattered across Instagram, WhatsApp groups, flyers and word of mouth.

Holistic Tribe Lombok brings these offerings together in one gentle place. It helps people who are already on the island find what resonates, and it helps people who are still planning their journey understand that Lombok has a growing holistic scene worth discovering.

By sharing the island’s facilitators, events and retreat spaces with care, we hope to attract more holistically interested travelers to Lombok — people who come not only to consume a destination, but to connect, practice, learn, heal and support the local community.

For visitors

A calm discovery guide for yoga, healing, bodywork, ceremonies, retreats and conscious community in Lombok.

For facilitators

A simple, elegant place to share your work, links, location, events and profile with people who are actively searching.

For Lombok

A way to support the island’s holistic ecosystem by making meaningful offerings easier to find and helping conscious visitors discover Lombok with more intention.

Next step
