About

About Ikara Yoga

Ikara Yoga focuses on the traditional Ashtanga method and presents itself as the first dedicated Traditional Ashtanga yoga studio and retreat in Lombok. Alongside regular practice, it also offers retreats and themed intensives for students wanting to go deeper.
