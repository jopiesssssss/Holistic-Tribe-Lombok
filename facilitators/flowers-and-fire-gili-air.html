About

About Flowers & Fire

Flowers & Fire runs yoga classes across two distinct island settings on Gili Air: a treehouse-style yoga garden and a seaside location. The approach is open and accessible, with an emphasis on soulful practice, breath and ease rather than performance.

✦
Two practice settings
Garden and seaside spaces create different atmospheres across the day.
✦
Accessible classes
The studio welcomes everyone from beginners to experienced yogis.
✦
Island rhythm
Workshops and special sessions sit naturally within Gili Air’s slower pace.
