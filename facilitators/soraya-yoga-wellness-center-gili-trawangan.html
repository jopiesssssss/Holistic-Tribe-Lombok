About

About Soraya Yoga

Soraya presents itself as an island sanctuary with a distinctive boutique feel. The property combines a wooden yoga shala, rooms with comfortable furnishings, a waterfall pool, a small health food café and a rooftop spa designed for mind-body reset.

✦
Sanctuary atmosphere
A stronger accommodation-and-wellness feel than a standard yoga studio.
✦
Holistic layering
Yoga sits alongside a café, pool and spa experiences.
✦
Central but calm
Useful for travelers who want softness and retreat energy while staying on Gili Trawangan.
