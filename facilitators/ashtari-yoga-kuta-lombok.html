About

About Ashtari Yoga & Retreat

Welcome to Ashtari Yoga in Kuta Lombok, your sanctuary for yoga, wellness and inner discovery. The venue sits among green hills with wide views of the sea, and the experience combines movement, mindfulness, nourishment and community in one elevated setting.
