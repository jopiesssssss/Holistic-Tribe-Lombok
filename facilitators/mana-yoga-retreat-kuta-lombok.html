About

About Mana Yoga Retreat

Mana is designed as a calm little oasis in the heart of Kuta Lombok. The official site combines accommodation with a yoga-and-retreat identity, while guest reviews repeatedly describe it as peaceful, beautiful and close enough to town while still feeling tucked away.

✦
Daily movement
Mana offers yoga every day and its own site and social feed also highlight Pilates, alongside practices for different moods and levels.
