About

About H2O Yoga & Meditation

H2O is one of Gili Air’s better-known all-round wellness bases, combining yoga, meditation and workshops with massage and other healing treatments. It also offers a café and accommodation-linked island experience, which makes it feel more like a small wellness hub than a single studio.
