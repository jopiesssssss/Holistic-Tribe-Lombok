About Tetebatu Retreats

This profile page is built around Tetebatu’s retreat appeal rather than one single large studio brand. The village is frequently described as a peaceful hill area in East Lombok, known for rice terraces, waterfalls, local culture and a slower rhythm that naturally suits yoga and meditation retreats.

Destination-based retreat energy
