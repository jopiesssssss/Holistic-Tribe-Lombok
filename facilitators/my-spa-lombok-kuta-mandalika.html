About My Spa Lombok

My Spa Lombok positions itself as a practical, accessible day spa in Kuta Mandalika. The menu spans classic massage therapy, body care, facial treatments and beauty services, making it a useful stop for both post-surf recovery and general self-care.

✦
Massage-first offering
Traditional Lombok massage, deep tissue, hot stone and reflexology are central to the menu.
✦
Beauty & skin care
Facials, waxing and grooming services broaden the offer beyond massage.
✦
Easy Kuta option
Convenient for travelers wanting straightforward wellness and beauty support in town.
