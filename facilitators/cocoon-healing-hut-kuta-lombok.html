About Cocoon Healing Lombok

Cocoon Healing Hut describes itself as a small sanctuary on a hill in Lombok, designed for people who want to slow down, reconnect and support well-being in body, mind and soul. The site says it is about ten minutes by scooter east of Kuta, with views over rice fields, villages and the sea.

✦
Retreat-ready setting
Cocoon explicitly positions itself as a venue where facilitators can host their own retreat in a calm, thoughtfully designed environment.
