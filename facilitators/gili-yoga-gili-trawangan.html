About

About Gili Yoga

Gili Yoga describes itself as a daily yoga and breathwork oasis in Gili Trawangan. Its schedule runs from morning to evening, while private sessions and a small accommodation offering help turn a class stop into a fuller island experience.

✦
Reliable daily schedule
Easy for travelers who want a clear morning or sunset class rhythm.
✦
Private class option
Breathwork and tailored one-on-one sessions add depth beyond drop-ins.
✦
Stay on site
Accommodation makes it easier to build a whole island routine around practice.
