About

About Slow Spa & Yoga

Slow Spa & Yoga is part of the wider SLOW Gili Air concept, a boutique luxury stay shaped around slower movement, rest and ease. The wellness side emphasizes personalized yoga or pilates sessions and elevated spa treatments rather than large public schedules.

✦
Spa-led atmosphere
Massage rooms and soaking rituals define the space as much as movement does.
✦
Private practice
Yoga and pilates are tailored to the individual rather than taught as crowded group classes.
✦
Strong design feel
The experience is aimed at guests who value aesthetics, comfort and a slower rhythm.
