About Sunset Beach Yoga

Flow by the ocean, fly high with the sunset.

Sunset Beach Yoga is a relaxed beach yoga space on Gili Trawangan, with classes shaped by ocean views, warm island air and a simple drop-in rhythm.

Start your day with Morning Flow by the ocean, or unwind in the evening with FlyHigh Yoga as the sun moves toward the horizon.

Good to know

Beginner-friendly and easy to join

✦
All levels welcome
All 1-hour adult classes are beginner-friendly and open to all levels.
✦
No booking needed
Just drop in. Please let them know in advance if you are coming as a group.
✦
Cash only
Currency exchange is available for Euros and USD. Cash only, 200k IDR per person.
