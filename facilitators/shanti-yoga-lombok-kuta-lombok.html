About

About Shanti Yoga Lombok

Located at Jivana Resort in Kuta Mandalika, Shanti Yoga Lombok combines daily classes with regular holistic events in a peaceful tropical setting. The studio positions itself as an accessible space for beginners, intermediate students and advanced practitioners alike.

✦
Daily classes
A regular schedule of yoga classes with equipment provided on site.

Known for sound healing, cacao ceremonies and special moon gatherings.
✦
Retreat atmosphere
A resort setting that works well for drop-ins, private sessions and hosted retreats.
