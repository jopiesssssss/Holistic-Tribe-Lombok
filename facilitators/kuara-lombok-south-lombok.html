About Kuara

Rooted in place and alive in spirit.

Kuara Lombok opened in June 2025 as an intimate, nature-led luxury resort on Bumbang Beach. The property is shaped by its setting: forest, sea, earth paths, open-air spaces and materials that feel connected to the island.

Every stay is designed to foster presence, cultural engagement and connection to land and community. It is a place to slow down, breathe deeper and experience a more grounded side of Lombok.

Experiences

Experiences that grow from the land

Kuara’s experiences are guided by the people who carry knowledge of this place: fishermen, foragers, cooks, guides and local hosts. Activities are hands-on, culturally rooted and shaped by the rhythm of the island.

Farm-to-table journeyGarden walks, local ingredients and a chef-led cooking atelier.
Kuara SpaMassage, botanicals, bodywork and quiet rituals inspired by island traditions.
Sand therapyA traditional healing ritual using warm, coral-rich beach sand.
Yoga in the gardenMovement, stillness and breath beneath the trees.
Coastal foragingLow-tide discovery guided by locals who know the shoreline.
Forest, village & waterBike tours, sacred springs, village visits and nature-led day experiences.
Stay & gather

Villas, retreats and private moments

With only 16 villas, Kuara offers privacy and calm for high-end travelers, couples, small groups and wellness-oriented stays. The property can also support intimate gatherings, retreat-style experiences and private takeovers.
